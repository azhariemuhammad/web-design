Project Pameri

We help anyone to free from Malaria

Why are we doing this?

Malaria remains a leading killer of children under 5 years old in many countries around the world. Project Pameri works closely with governments to scale up proven interventions and improve the effectiveness of malaria programs so children no longer die from this preventable and treatable disease. We believe malaria elimination is possible, and is working with partners around the world towards achieving this goal.
